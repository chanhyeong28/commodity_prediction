# Mitsui Commodity Prediction Challenge Specific Prompts
# Prompts tailored for the Mitsui commodity prediction dataset

## Dataset Description
Mitsui commodity price prediction challenge
Commodity futures price forecasting
Multi-variate commodity time series prediction
Financial commodity market analysis
Trading strategy optimization
Risk management for commodity portfolios
Supply and demand forecasting
Market sentiment analysis
Economic indicator correlation
Seasonal commodity patterns

## Task Descriptions for Different Prediction Horizons

### 96-step prediction (short-term)
forecast the next 96 steps given the previous 96 steps information
predict next 96 time periods of commodity prices
short-term commodity price forecasting
intraday commodity price prediction
immediate market trend analysis

### 192-step prediction (medium-term)
forecast the next 192 steps given the previous 96 steps information
predict next 192 time periods of commodity prices
medium-term commodity price forecasting
weekly commodity price prediction
market cycle analysis

### 336-step prediction (long-term)
forecast the next 336 steps given the previous 96 steps information
predict next 336 time periods of commodity prices
long-term commodity price forecasting
monthly commodity price prediction
seasonal trend analysis

### 720-step prediction (very long-term)
forecast the next 720 steps given the previous 96 steps information
predict next 720 time periods of commodity prices
very long-term commodity price forecasting
quarterly commodity price prediction
macro-economic trend analysis
